Hey there!

AweChords A Cappella is a co-ed group at UCLA that strives to share our passion for music with the greater Los Angeles community. We were founded in 2006 as a musical component in UCLAÊ¼s annual Vietnamese Culture Night (VCN). In 2010, we began to diversify our sound and regrouped to form AweChords A Cappella. We continue to perform at VCN every year for an audience of over 2,000 people.

Our repertoire ranges from pop to rock to show tunes, and we are constantly working on new pieces to share with our audiences. Above all, AweChords is a home away from home. We aim to create life-long bonds that will last years after we have finished our last performance.
